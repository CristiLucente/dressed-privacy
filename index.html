Dressed: AI Personal Stylist - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Dressed collects the following: email address and password for account creation, photos and descriptions of clothing items, style preferences, body type, budget, location settings, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide personalized outfit recommendations, manage your wardrobe, process subscriptions, and improve the App.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud services with encryption in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (authentication and storage), RevenueCat (subscription management), and Apple (In-App Purchase processing).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile section within the App.
